Some refrigerator problems and their solutions:
01. Leaking Water is one of the most common Refrigerator Problems
This is also potentially a hazard, as one can slip and fall due to leaking water if left unnoticed. There can be two causes of these refrigerator problems. The first reason could be blocked defrost drain and another possible reason can be clogged or frozen water supply. You can fix this by draining the frost drain located at the back of your freezer with warm water. You can also manually clean the rubber valve located inside the defrost drain.
02. Refrigerator not Cooling
Sometimes you might experience that your refrigerator is not maintaining the temperatures that it should. The primary reason behind this is a dirty condenser coil and you need to clean the coil to fix this.

The condenser coil is located at the bottom of your refrigerator. Don’t forget to unplug it before doing this. You can also do this on regular basis as part of its maintenance.
03. Refrigerator Door not Shutting Properly
If you have been using it for so long then you must have experienced that the rubber seal of the door is not holding the door intact. This can cause other problems in your refrigerator and might even increase energy consumption. To solve this refrigerator problem, you will need to get the rubber seal replaced.
04. Frost Build up Inside your Refrigerator
The freezer unit of the refrigerator acts as a storage space for ice and frozen food. If you observe that significant frost or ice is building up inside it, it might adversely impact the proper functioning of the freezer. This problem is caused by increased humidity inside the freezer. It might be because of leaving the freezer door open for too long or due to a faulty door seal. You will need to get the seal replaced in this case as well.
05. Noisy Refrigerator
They do make a little noise as part of their normal functioning. But if the noise from your refrigerator has increased significantly it might be caused by a malfunctioning fan. It consists of two types of fans: a condenser fan and an evaporator fan. These fans keep the condenser coil cool and help to maintain an adequate temperature inside the refrigerator. One of the probable causes of a noisy refrigerator is a faulty motor in one of these fans. You will need to call an expert to fix this problem for you.
06. Refrigerator is Warm
The freezer unit of the refrigerator acts as a storage space for ice and frozen food. If you observe that significant frost or ice is building up inside it, it might adversely impact the proper functioning of the freezer. This problem is caused by increased humidity inside the freezer. It might be because of leaving the freezer door open for too long or due to a faulty door seal. You will need to get the seal replaced in this case as well.
07. High Electricity Consumption
If you notice a sudden spike in your electricity bill it might be because of the refrigerator. This is because it is one of the most energy-intensive appliances at home. You can use an energy meter to measure the electricity consumption of your refrigerator. If the sudden increase in electricity bill is indeed due to the refrigerator, you will need to book a professional service to solve this problem.
08. Poor temperature control
If your top shelves seem warmer than the bottom in the fridge compartment or the temperature appears fine in the morning but deteriorates during the day is one of the more common problems we see. This is often symptomatic of an underlying issue which requires intervention to ensure safe storage temperatures.
09. Thermostat Replacement
If your refrigerator (fridge and freezer) stops running we can replace the thermostat with a genuine manufacturers part for optimal reliability.
10. Ice on the floor of the freezer
A frequent fault which usually gets worse and the expanding ice can be potentially very destructive. We can eliminate the cause and save lasting damage.
